About Me

I received the B.S. degrees in Mathematics from Nanjing University (NJU) in 2017. I am now a last year Ph.D. candidate (Sept 2017 - July 2022) in Department of Probability and Statistics, School of Mathematical Sciences, Peking University, Beijing, China. My supervisor is Prof. Minghua Deng. I was a research intern (Nov 2019 - Sept 2021) in DAMO Academy, Alibaba Group, supervised by Dr. Chong Chen. I am also a research intern in MSRA now.
