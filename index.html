About Me

I'm currently a postdoctoral researcher in Department of Computer Science, University of California, Los Angeles, under the supervision of Prof. Yizhou Sun. I received the B.S. degree in Mathematics from Nanjing University (NJU) in 2017 and the Ph.D. degree in Statistics from Peking University (PKU) in 2022 under the supervision of Prof. Minghua Deng. I was a research intern in DAMO Academy, Alibaba Group, advised by Dr. Chong Chen. I was also a research intern in MSRA.
