About

I'm currently a postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Computer Science, UCLA, under the supervision of Prof. Yizhou Sun. I received the Ph.D. degree in Statistics from the School of Mathematical Sciences, Peking University (PKU) in 2022 under the supervision of Prof. Minghua Deng and the B.S. degree in Statistics from the Department of Mathematics, Nanjing University (NJU) in 2017. I was a research intern in DAMO Academy, Alibaba Group and Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA).

I have been collaborating with Prof. Hongyu Zhao (Ira V. Hiscock Professor of Biostatistics, Yale), Prof. Xiting Yan (Associate Professor of Medicine, Yale), Prof. Ming Zhang (Professor of Computer Science, PKU) and Dr. Xian-Sheng Hua (CTO, Terminus Group, IEEE Fellow).

I have authored over 60 publications in prestigious venues including NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR, ICCV, CVPR, TheWebConf, TMLR, AAAI, IJCAI, ICDM, TPAMI (IF=23.6), Information Fusion (IF=18.6), TIP (IF=10.6), TNNLS (IF=10.4), TKDE (IF=8.9), TKDD (IF=3.6), TOMM (IF=5.1) and Bioinformatics (IF=5.8).

I'm on the Editorial Board of BMC Bioinformatics (Rank #4 in Bioinformatics & Computational Biology from Google Scholar Metric), served as an Area Chair for ACM MM 2024 and served as the PC Member for most prestigious machine learning conferences and three high impact journals (IF>10).

I am on the 2023-2024 job market! Feel free to connect me!

Research Interests

My research focuses on the intersection of Machine Learning, Statistical Modeling and Scientific Problems (AI/ML for Science including Biology, Genomics, Health, Mechanics and Information Science). Generally, I work on building computational models for interdisciplinary scientific applications. The ultimate goal of my research is to allow every researcher in any scientific field to benefit from machine learning and data mining. I have a special focus on learning over dynamical systems, data-efficient graph machine learning, and health/biology-related real-world applications.
