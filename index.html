About Me

I'm currently a postdoctoral researcher in Department of Computer Science, University of California, Los Angeles, under the supervision of Prof. Yizhou Sun. I received the Ph.D. degree in Statistics from the School of Mathematical Sciences, Peking University (PKU) in 2022 under the supervision of Prof. Minghua Deng and the B.S. degree in Statistics from the Department of Mathematics, Nanjing University (NJU) in 2017. I was a research intern in DAMO Academy, Alibaba Group, advised by Dr. Chong Chen. I was also a research intern in MSRA.

I am on the 2023-2024 job market! Feel free to connect me!
